Welcome to Dystoria

Create stories with a world-generating engine using prompts and mindmaps. A Story Wiki grows from your writing, with summaries of each element and of every session.

Signed out, stories save in this browser — use Back Up or Auto-Export in the menu to keep copies on your computer.
