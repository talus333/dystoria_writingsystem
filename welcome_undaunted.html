Where the AI stands

A tool for the author, not the author.

We do not believe a story should be written by a machine. Strip away the noise and AI is a pattern recognizer, and pointed at your own work it becomes a lens: it shows you what you have written and helps you order it, and it never writes a sentence in your place.

It is opt-in and hideable, tucked behind a single star. Turn it off and the whole studio still works, because the structure was never the AI’s to begin with. When you reach for it, it clears the busywork and sharpens your sight, so the vision, the voice, and every real decision stay yours.

Stage by stage, here is exactly where it helps.
Plan

Structure from the scatter

Turn scattered notes into elements and place them on the map automatically. Ask any element Socratic questions that reveal more as you answer, and let your notes update themselves. Explore an element to surface hidden connections and fresh possibilities.

Write

A spark that knows your story

Prompts and ideas for where to go next, drawn from your notes, the prose so far, and the few elements you have chosen to focus on for this session.

Refine

See your own craft

Deterministic style checks reflect your rhythm back to you. Refract holds a sentence up so you can rewrite it stronger in your own words. Deeper story analysis reads pacing, threads and gaps, and never touches a word itself.

Review

Hear it whole

A clear summary lets you feel how the whole story sounds, and soon you will be able to sit back and listen to it read aloud.

Wiki

Order, and the next question

Your notes and prose gathered into a clean, living wiki that raises the most important questions still open in your story, and points you to similar, highly regarded books so you can see the tradition your story lives in.

Import

Start from what you have

Bring in notes or prose you have already written and Dystoria ingests it, drawing out the elements, sections and connections on its own, so an existing story gets a running start.

Every prompt is a suggestion. Every suggestion is yours to take, bend, or throw away. You are the one telling the story, and the tool only helps you tell it well.

What it costs

Everything a writer needs, free.

Dystoria isn’t a place to pay to write, because the writing is yours. The whole studio for planning, getting unstuck, drafting, refining, sharing, and gathering feedback is free. All features will be available for free, but the AI-supported features will have limitations. We will add a paid tier later, for users who want to go deep with AI and need unlimited capabilities and more powerful models.
