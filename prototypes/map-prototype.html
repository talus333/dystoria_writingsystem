Dystoria — map prototype

Pick a land type and sketch a rough area on the map — it smooths and fills with watercolor + features. Draw another over it to replace and blend. Choose Select to drag pawns between places.

Tip: choose Forest, then drag-draw a loop on the map.

Texture dials

Drag a slider and any terrain you've already drawn redraws live. Current value shows at the right of each label.

Pawn: pseudo-3D (SVG)

Maya and Corin on the map. A cast shadow + a shaded body and a lit head read as 3D — vector, single-file, instant to restyle. Recommended.

Pawn: real 3D (Three.js)

The same token as real geometry — drag to rotate. Truer depth, but heavier and breaks single-file. Only worth it for a tilt-and-orbit world.
