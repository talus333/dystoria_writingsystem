The Map

Your world, laid out where you can see it.

Roll for a cast or place your own. Beings, settings, objects, and events become hand-drawn tokens on an endless map — then you wire them together until the shape of the story appears.

Roll & Gather

Summon a cast and a world from deep, curated word-pools — or add your own. Every element lands on the map as its own illustrated token.

Wire the Relationships

Draw a line between any two elements and name the bond, both ways. Faint until you look — then the whole web lights up.

Thread the Storylines

Gather beings, stakes, and events into colored threads and groups. Highlight one and the rest of the map recedes, so a single arc reads at a glance.
